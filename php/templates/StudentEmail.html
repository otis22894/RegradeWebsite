Please do not submit a duplicate regrade request unless specifically instructed to do so by your TA. While the deadline to submit a regrade is one week after the date that the grades are released, the deadline for regrades to be processed is not. As long as you submit a regrade within one week of the grades being released, your regrade will not be denied for time reasons, even if it is not processed within one week. If you would like to know the status of your regrade request, please talk to your TA in person.
